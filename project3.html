VARIETIES

Totapuri

Totapuri mango is famous mango variety in Karnataka state,India. it is know for its unique shape,which is elongated & pointed.

Alphonso

Alphonso mango is one of the most popular mango varieties in Karnataka. it is know for its sweet taste, juicy pulp, & vibrant color.

Neelam

Neelam mango is a popular mango variety in Karnataka that is known for its juicy pulp. it is used to make dessert,smoothies,& shakes.

Banganpalli

Banganpalli mango is a popular variety of mango that is grown in the state of Karnataka. know for its sweet & juicy flavor.

Raspuri

Raspuri mango is a popular mango variety in Karnataka that is known for its juicy pulp.it is used to make dessert,smoothies,& shakes.
